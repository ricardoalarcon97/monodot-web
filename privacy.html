Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Data Minimization

monoDot is designed to know as little about you as possible. We do not sell your personal data.

2. Note Storage & Sync

  • Guest Users: Your notes are stored exclusively on your device's local storage. We have no access to them.
  • Account Holders: If you create an account, your notes are encrypted and stored securely to allow synchronization between your devices.

3. AI & Voice Processing

To provide intelligent features, certain data is processed:

  • Text Analysis: When you use AI features (Auto-Reminders, Deep Assistance), the text of that specific interaction is processed by our AI provider. This data is not used to train models.
  • Voice Transcriptions: Audio data for "Voice-to-Text" is processed temporarily to convert it to text. The audio file is not permanently stored on our servers after transcription is complete.

4. Advertising (Basic Plan)

For users on the Basic plan, we display third-party advertisements. These ad networks may use anonymous identifiers (like IDFA, subject to your permission) to show relevant ads. Upgrading to Pro removes all ads and stops this data usage.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at privacy@monodot.lat.
