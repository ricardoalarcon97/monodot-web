Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Welcome to monoDot. We believe your thoughts are private. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

monoDot is a privacy-first application. We do not collect, store, or read your personal notes. All content is stored locally on your device and synchronized exclusively via your personal iCloud account.

2. Payments & Financial Data

monoDot may offer paid subscriptions via Apple In-App Purchases.

  • We do not process or store your credit card information.
  • All transactions are handled securely by Apple securely directly.
  • We only receive a receipt token to verify your subscription status.

3. Third Party Services

We may use anonymous telemetry to improve app stability (crashes). No identifiable content is shared.

Support Center

Frequently asked questions and direct contact.

Does monoDot work offline?

Yes. monoDot is 100% native and works perfectly offline. AI features require internet to process, but writing is always available.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the app Settings > tap "Restore Purchases". Ensure you are logged in with the same Apple ID used to buy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

We cannot cancel it for you. You must go to your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > monoDot > Cancel.

Are my notes private?

Yes. We cannot see them. They live on your device and your iCloud.
