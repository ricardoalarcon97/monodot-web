Terms of Service

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Accounts & Data Persistence

monoDot allows you to use the app without creating an account ("Guest Mode").

  • Guest Users: If you use the app without an account, your notes are stored locally on your iOS device. Warning: If you delete the app, restore your phone, or lose your device, your notes will be permanently lost. We cannot recover them.
  • Registered Users: To ensure data safety and cloud synchronization, you must create an account. This allows you to retrieve your notes on a new device.

2. Service Tiers & Limitations

The app offers two tiers of service with specific usage limits:

monoDot Basic (Free)
  • Unlimited manual notes & manual reminders.
  • Ads: Third-party advertisements will be displayed.
  • AI Deep Assistance: Limited to 2 calls per day.
  • AI Auto-Reminders: Limited to 5 automatic detections per day.
  • Context Window: Maximum 10 messages per AI conversation.
  • Voice-to-Text: Not available.
monoDot Pro (Subscription)
  • Everything unlimited.
  • No Ads.
  • Unlimited AI Assistance & Reminders.
  • Full access to Voice-to-Text transcriptions.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple via the App Store.

  • No Account Required for Payment: You do not need a monoDot account to subscribe; the purchase is linked to your Apple ID.
  • Auto-Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Cancellation: You must cancel via your iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. monoDot cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf.

4. User Conduct

You agree not to misuse the AI features to generate illegal, harmful, or abusive content. We reserve the right to suspend access to AI features if abusive patterns are detected.
